DocuMind ingestion smoke fixture.

This small plain-text document exists only so the Phase-2 e2e test can upload a
real file, watch a document row appear in the project view, and poll its status
pill until it reaches a terminal state (ready or failed). The content is
intentionally short — a few sentences are enough to produce at least one chunk
and one embedding through the ingestion pipeline (parse -> chunk -> embed ->
store) so the happy path is exercised end to end.
